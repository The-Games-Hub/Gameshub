What the hell is "The Archive"?

Hey there, I'm NCGamer118, a coding enthusiast and middle schooler. My journey into the world of coding began in 6th grade when I had the amazing opportunity to collaborate with winrar. Together, we embarked on countless coding adventures, laying the foundation for what would become "The Games Hub". "The Games Hub", our brainchild, quickly became a go-to destination for unblocked games that gained immense popularity. As our team expanded, we welcomed talented individuals like Cyber_Files_X, Suddam, GamerStorm, 3kh0, TitanGamer, A_Person, Crouching_Sharkk, Clicky, Otto and Exavier3683. However, the path was not always smooth. Our beloved site faced blocks and hurdles, and we had to constantly relocate to keep it alive. Then, in January 2024, Replit discontinued its free hosting, leading to the disbandment of "The Games Hub". But, as they say, every ending is a new beginning. On March 25, 2024, armed with the incredible website template crafted by 3kh0, I proudly launched "The Archive." This new website is a testament to our resilience and determination, offering a fresh platform for users to explore and enjoy. Join me on this exciting journey as we continue to push boundaries and explore the limitless possibilities of coding. Welcome to "The Archive," where the spirit of gaming lives on!
